FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ???

Can a person with same mail id enroll as student as well as coach ?

No a person with same mail id cannot register as both student as well as coach.

What is the use of clicking that team up button ?

By clicking on the team up button for a particular coach you will get an email notification as soon as that particular coach uploads a video.

Can I enroll to multiple coaches ?

Yes a person can enroll to multiple coaches.

Can I teach multiple sports ?

No you cannot teach multiple sports.

What is the maximum video size I can upload ?

The maximum file size limit is 15 MB.
